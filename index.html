● Pay for items in the Shopping District at the price listed by the owner. If there is some confusion on what to pay and how much to take you can ask another online player, post
in discord or to check the listed price in the google Docs page here.

● No Squatting. You are not allowed to move into someone else’s base or builds without their permission. If you have any questions about the rules please post them on the discord server or comment below.
